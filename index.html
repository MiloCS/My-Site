ABOUT ME

I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst, studying computer science. I originally hail from Cambridge, Massachusetts, and attended Cambridge Rindge and Latin High School. In my free time, I like to do random stuff like building this website. You can check out some of the other projects I've created under the projects tab.

I originally became interested in programming when I was introduced to the block language Scratch in middle school, and I have since expanded both my interest and my knowledge in computer science significantly.


I am now familiar with many different programming languages and paradigms, including (but not limited to) Python, Java, Scala, C, C#, and Javascript (Including React and Node.js) I'll admit that I often have to Google how to use syntax in any and all of these languages while I'm programming, but I think that's one of the things that makes me a better programmer - I'm always looking to learn more.

In the time I have outside of my classes and personal projects (which isn't much), I watch lots of YouTube and Netflix. While at school, I am a member of the UMass Mock Trial Team, and will be taking on the role of VP of Finance for the team for the 2019-2020 season.
